How to Play

Your goal: Fill every playable square with the right letters so the grid makes real, criss‑crossing words.

Getting started

  • The letters sit around the outside of the board in little tiles.
  • Click a letter to pick it up, then click a square to put it down.

Where letters can go

  • Letters on the left or right edges can go anywhere along that same line across.
  • Letters on the top or bottom edges can go anywhere along that same line down.
  • Only the bold, solid‑border squares are playable. The faint, dashed ones are just empty space.

Changed your mind?

  • Click a filled square to send that letter back to where it started.
  • You can keep moving letters until everything fits.

When you’ve got it

  • The game stays quiet while you work.
  • When every playable square is filled and all letters are correct, game over.

Settings

  • Size: Pick a small, medium, or large board. This makes a new puzzle.
  • Difficulty: Easier means placements feel more obvious; harder means they’re trickier. Changing this makes a new puzzle.

Quick tip: Press Esc to close this window, or click outside it.
