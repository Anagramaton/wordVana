How to Play

OBJECTIVE: REASSEMBLE THE BOARD USING THE LETTER TILES TO FIND THE CORRECT SOLUTION.

GETTIN STARTED

  • ALL OUTSIDE LETTER TILES MAKE UP ALL THE INSIDE WORDS.
  • CLICK A LETTER TO HIGHLIGHT THE TILE, CLICK AN AVAILABLE SLOT ON THE BOARD TO DROP IT.

DESTINATION DIRECTION

  • LETTERS ON THE LEFT or RIGHT BORDER CAN GO INTO ANY AVAILABLE CELL ALONG ITS ROW.
  • LETTERS ON THE TOP or BOTTOM BORDER CAN GO INTO ANY AVAILABLE CELL ALONG ITS COLUMN.
  • USE THE PROCESS OF ELIMINATION TO FIGURE OUT WHICH TILE BELONGS TO WHICH AVAILABLE CELL.

CHANGE YOUR MIND?

  • CLICK A FILLED SQUARE TO SEND THAT LETTER BACK TO WHERE IT ONCE CAME.
  • MOVE LETTERS BACK AND FORTH UNTIL EVERY LETTER TILE IS USED.
