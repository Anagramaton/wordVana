How to Play

OBJECTIVE: Reassemble the board using the outside letter tiles.

  • Letters on the left or right border can be placed anywhere in that row.
  • Letters on the top or bottom border can be placed anywhere in that column.
  • Only playable cells accept letters.
  • Click a placed letter to send it back.
  • Use elimination until every letter fits.

When everything locks in — you’ll know.
